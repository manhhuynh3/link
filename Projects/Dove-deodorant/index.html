Dove Deodorant Night Serum - 3D Product Video

This project showcases a 3D video for Dove Deodorant Night Serum, created using Blender for 3D modeling and After Effects for post-production. The video focuses on highlighting the product’s nighttime functionality, particularly its ability to restore skin damaged after hair removal.
